Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

Quick, garlicky, and full of flavor!

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 4 cloves garlic (sliced)
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp chili flakes
  • Salt to taste
